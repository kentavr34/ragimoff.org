About the Specialist

Kenan Ragimov

Psychotherapist (IPAS Ambassador). 23 years of clinical practice.

  • 2026 Authored the Clinical Psychiatry handbook — a comprehensive reference covering diagnosis and treatment of psychological disorders, updated for 2026 — Read the handbook →
  • 2022 Received the certificate of Ambassador of the International Psychotherapy Association and international-level psychotherapist with the right to teach in this field
  • 2021 Appointed as lecturer at the St. Petersburg Institute of Continuing Education (Russia) and authorised representative of the B. D. Karvasarsky Institute
  • 2008 Founder of GTC and later RAGIMOFF companies, business consultant to numerous projects — conducted over 3,000 professional training sessions
  • 2003 As director of the Baku Regional Psychology Centre, led social projects with TV and radio channels to develop psychology in Azerbaijan
  • 2003 Awarded the Professional Psychotherapist Diploma with distinction and an expert certificate from the V. M. Bekhterev Research Institute
  • 2001 Trained under the chief psychotherapist of the Russian Federation, Professor B. D. Karvasarsky; worked under the guidance of world-renowned psychoanalyst Professor V. D. Vidin; completed a 2-year clinical residency
