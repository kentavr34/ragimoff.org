ABOUT THE PROGRAMME

What is the Psychotherapy Practicum?

Live sessions, supervision, case conference format — for those who already know the theory.
International IPAS/BPA certificate, QR-verified. 864 hours.

The Psychotherapy Practicum is designed for professionals who have completed a foundational psychology course and have a solid theoretical base, but lack the confidence to work with real clients. The programme minimises theory and maximises practice.

The programme runs for 12 months at 72 hours per month: 36 hours of live sessions (role-plays, case analysis, case conferences) + 36 hours of video recordings. Total: 864 hours. Additionally, 2 individual supervision sessions are held monthly throughout the year.

Those who successfully complete the programme receive an international IPAS (International Psychotherapy Accreditation Society) and BPA certificate — a QR-verified document recognised outside Azerbaijan.

  • A foundational psychology course is required (mandatory prerequisite)
  • Groups of maximum 8 participants — each receives individual attention
  • Live demonstration of real sessions with Dr. Ragimov
  • Practice conducting your own sessions under supervision
  • 2 individual supervision sessions per month throughout the year
  • Accumulating hours towards the IPAS/BPA certificate

PRACTICE FORMATS

5 Working Formats

Each format is designed to build independent clinical skills.

Format 01

Role-Plays

Participants take turns in the roles of psychologist and client. The supervisor provides real-time feedback. The most effective format for practising the dynamics of a real session.

Format 02

Case Analysis

Group discussion of real (anonymised) clinical cases. Diagnostic thinking, treatment planning, and exploring alternative approaches.

Format 03

Case Conference

Multidisciplinary discussion of complex cases from the perspectives of a psychiatrist, psychologist, and psychotherapist. Building collaborative decision-making skills.

Format 04

Video Archive

Access to over 100 recordings of real sessions. Observe how different psychotherapeutic approaches look in practice. For independent self-study.

Format 05

Final Assessment

A 30-minute real case presentation before a panel. History, diagnosis, treatment plan, prognosis. Those who pass receive the IPAS/BPA certificate.
