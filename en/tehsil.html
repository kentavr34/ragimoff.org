Official Documents

CPD Diploma & International Certificate

Upon completing the programme, graduates receive an official Diploma
and certificates from the St. Petersburg Institute of Continuing Education.

Legal Status of the Diploma

Under the Agreement between the Russian Federation and the Republic of Azerbaijan in the field of education (AR Law No. 455-IIQ of 13 May 2003), the diploma of the St. Petersburg Institute of Continuing Education is an officially recognised document in Azerbaijan. Graduates — subject to employer requirements — may work in both state and private institutions.
